JimJobs


JimJobs allows you to document your job search journey through objects for Companies, Contacts, and Positions. From the main dashboard page, use the forms to enter in information and see it reflected in realtime. Visit the corresponding index page for each model to see everything you've submitted.

Short, sweet, and straight to the point.

This is my fourth and final project as a Flatiron School student.

FoodView


With FoodView2, you finally have a legitimate reason to post pictures of your food on the internet. You can rate each dish you create and also see Dishes created by other people. You can also add your own restaurants, or use those created by others. This include all the awesomeness of the original FoodView, but with asynchronous JS functions incuded. The restaurant index and show pages have been converted into one all-encompassing Restaurants page, but otherwise it's the same app.

This is my third project as a Flatiron School student.

MisterFitness


With MisterFitness you can log your workouts online to keep track of your athletic progress over time. You can also share your workouts with your friends to add some friendly competition! MisterFitness even makes it easy to duplicate a friend's workout for an added convenience. Each workout is composed of exercises that are created from scratch or cloned from existing exercises.
